The Best Animated Movies According to Ian

My top 3 animated movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away follows a 10-year-old girl who must save her parents from the spirit realm and an evil witch who runs a spirit bathhouse. still my top movie of all time by Hayao Miyazaki.

Akira

Directed by Katsuhiro Otomo, Akira's violent yet beautiful visuals are to this day iconic and have been directly or indirectly referenced in many other films and TV shows.

Grave of the Fireflies (1988)

A movie from Studio Ghibli and directed by Isao Takahata. The film follows two children struggling to survive in wartorn 1940's Japan.
